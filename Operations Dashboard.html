SQL & Analytics

Example Queries (SQLite‑style SQL)

The Excel/Sheets dataset is loaded into a lightweight SQLite database, enabling flexible filtering and aggregation before feeding a BI tool.

Filter by Date, Job Type, and Status

Pull just the jobs relevant for a production review within a specific time window and operational focus. 

-- Completed fabrication jobs in Q1 2025
SELECT
    Job_ID,
    Customer_Type,
    Job_Type,
    Start_Date,
    End_Date,
    Hours_Worked,
    Material_Cost,
    Labor_Cost,
    Revenue
FROM jobs
WHERE
    date(Start_Date) >= '2025-01-01'
    AND date(End_Date)   < '2025-04-01'
    AND Job_Type         = 'Fabrication'
    AND Status           = 'Completed';

Aggregate by Month, Job Type, and Customer Type

Roll up volumes and dollars to see mix and profitability trends. 

-- Monthly revenue and jobs by job type
SELECT
    strftime('%Y-%m', End_Date) AS Month,
    Job_Type,
    COUNT(*)                    AS Jobs_Completed,
    SUM(Revenue)                AS Total_Revenue,
    SUM(Material_Cost + Labor_Cost) AS Total_Cost,
    SUM(Revenue) - SUM(Material_Cost + Labor_Cost) AS Gross_Margin
FROM jobs
WHERE Status = 'Completed'
GROUP BY Month, Job_Type
ORDER BY Month, Job_Type;

CTE: Monthly Revenue & Jobs Completed

A reusable summary table that can be joined to other dimensions or referenced directly by BI visuals. 

WITH monthly_summary AS (
    SELECT
        strftime('%Y-%m', End_Date) AS Month,
        COUNT(*)                    AS Jobs_Completed,
        SUM(Revenue)                AS Revenue,
        SUM(Material_Cost + Labor_Cost) AS Cost,
        SUM(Revenue) - SUM(Material_Cost + Labor_Cost) AS Margin
    FROM jobs
    WHERE Status = 'Completed'
    GROUP BY strftime('%Y-%m', End_Date)
)
SELECT
    Month,
    Jobs_Completed,
    Revenue,
    Cost,
    Margin,
    ROUND(Margin * 1.0 / NULLIF(Cost, 0) * 100, 1) AS Margin_Percent
FROM monthly_summary
ORDER BY Month;

Dashboard Design

Visualization Tool

Dashboard built in Power BI Desktop (can also be replicated in Tableau Public or Looker Studio), with a single page focused on throughput, profitability, and reliability.

Key Visuals

  • Jobs per Month – column chart using the monthly summary to show completed jobs and seasonality in workload.
  • Revenue vs. Cost per Month – clustered columns or combo chart showing revenue, total cost, and gross margin trend.
  • On‑Time Completion Rate – card or line chart showing the % of completed jobs that met their promised due date.
  • Job Mix by Type / Customer Segment – stacked bars or donut charts to compare volume and revenue mix.

Interactivity

  • Slice by date range, customer type, job type, and status.
  • Highlight specific job types to see their impact on revenue and margin.
  • Drill from monthly summaries down to individual jobs when needed.
This project can be extended with additional metrics (WIP, quote‑to‑win rate, or machine utilization) by adding columns to the source dataset and exposing them through new SQL views and dashboard visuals.
